About Us

The gig economy has come to usurp the labor market in the US; with a third of the US labor force being part-time/full-time gig workers. The upsurge in the demand is not matched by the availability of resources & capabilities to drive the gig economy even better. This conundrum has led to us at Gigzonwheels LLC, wanting to create a solution in our own little way by providing opportunities for people to make money on their own terms.
With Omaha being an untapped high demand area in the Mid-west, we are handing Omaha residents the keys to success on the road. Lets get you that gig!
